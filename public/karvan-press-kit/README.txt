KARAVAN PRESS KIT
=================

Thank you for downloading the Karavan Press Kit.

This package contains official Karavan logo files in various formats and resolutions,
optimized for both digital and print use.


FILE INVENTORY:
--------------

LOGO VARIANTS:

• karavan-logo.svg
  - Vector format (scalable to any size)
  - Brand blue color
  - Best for: Web, apps, digital use

• karavan-logo-brand-blue.png
  - PNG format, standard resolution
  - Brand blue color on transparent background
  - Best for: General digital use, presentations

• karavan-logo-white.png
  - PNG format, standard resolution
  - White color on transparent background
  - Best for: Dark backgrounds, overlays

HIGH-RESOLUTION VERSIONS:

• karavan-logo-brand-4x.png
  - 4x resolution PNG
  - Brand blue color
  - Best for: Large displays, banners

• karavan-logo-white-4x.png
  - 4x resolution PNG
  - White color
  - Best for: Large displays on dark backgrounds

• karavan-logo-brand-hr.png
  - High-resolution PNG
  - Brand blue color
  - Best for: Print materials, high-quality publications

• karavan-logo-white-hr.png
  - High-resolution PNG
  - White color
  - Best for: Print on dark backgrounds

SYMBOL ONLY (Icon):

• karavan-symbol-brand-blue.png
  - Symbol/icon only, brand blue
  - Best for: App icons, favicons, small spaces

• karavan-symbol-white.png
  - Symbol/icon only, white
  - Best for: Small spaces on dark backgrounds


USAGE GUIDELINES:
----------------

1. DO use the logo as provided without modification
2. DO maintain proper spacing around the logo
3. DO NOT stretch, rotate, or distort the logo
4. DO NOT change the colors or add effects
5. DO NOT use the logo on cluttered or low-contrast backgrounds
